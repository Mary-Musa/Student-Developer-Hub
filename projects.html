Problem It Solves

Rural schools often lack access to digital learning tools. This website aims to bridge that gap by providing resources, guidance, and information for digital education initiatives.

My Approach

I created a multi-page HTML5 site with structured navigation, clear content sections, and high-quality media to showcase initiatives like digital classrooms and teacher training.

The Hardest Thing I Learned

The biggest challenge was structuring a multi-page site so that navigation is intuitive and content is accessible to all users, including those with minimal technical background.
